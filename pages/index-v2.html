Weddings & Celebrations

A celebration of love, designed around you

Nestled between Ludwigshafen, Speyer and Mannheim — right on the idyllic “Blue Adriatic” — Hotel Darstein is more than a venue. It is a complete lakeside wedding destination where the ceremony, reception, dinner, party and overnight stay all happen in one romantic place.

From the first viewing to the morning-after breakfast, our experienced team carries the details so you can simply be present. Beautiful by the water, warm in every season, and effortless from start to finish.

  • Wedding island & lakefront ceremony
  • Hotel rooms for you & your guests
  • Restaurant-quality catering in-house
  • Calm, complete planning support
