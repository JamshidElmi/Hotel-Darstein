Below is a **wedding-first homepage section plan** for **Hotel Darstein**, with other services treated as secondary pathways. The strategy should position Darstein less like a general hotel and more like a **romantic lakeside wedding location at the Blue Adriatic**, similar in focus to Haldmühle’s wedding-first approach. Haldmühle leads with clear wedding-location promises like rain alternative, indoor/outdoor dinner, 20–170 guests, and romantic positioning, while Hotel Darstein’s strongest differentiator is the **lakefront setting, wedding island, hotel rooms, restaurant, and full event infrastructure in one place**. ([Haldmühle Bissersheim][1])

## Must-Have Above-the-Fold Elements

The top of the homepage should include: a romantic lake/wedding image or video, headline focused on weddings, one sentence about the location, two CTAs, and 4–5 trust/differentiator bullets.

Example structure:

**Headline:**
“Your Dream Wedding Location Directly on the Blue Adriatic”

**Subheadline:**
“Celebrate your wedding by the lake with ceremony, reception, dinner, party, and guest accommodation in one romantic location near Mannheim, Ludwigshafen and Speyer.”

**Primary CTA:**
“Request Wedding Viewing”

**Secondary CTA:**
“View Wedding Packages”

**Quick bullets:**
Wedding island, lakeside reception, indoor/outdoor celebration spaces, hotel rooms for guests, menu & planning support.

## Best Strategic Positioning

Hotel Darstein should not compete only as a “hotel.” The homepage should position it as:

**A complete lakeside wedding destination**
where couples can hold the ceremony, reception, dinner, party, photos, and overnight stay in one place.

That is the strongest angle because Haldmühle focuses heavily on romantic event-location appeal, while Darstein can combine **romance + lake + hotel convenience + catering + full event service**. Haldmühle’s homepage highlights emotional wedding-location benefits like romantic setting, rain alternative, indoor/outdoor dinner, and guest range; Darstein should match that clarity while leaning harder into the lakefront and hotel advantages.
